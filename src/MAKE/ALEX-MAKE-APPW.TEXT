$EXEC
{Builds the Preferences Window(s) and installs it as tool #11.}

$SUBMIT ALEX/ASM/APPW {Assemble all assembly language source files}
$SUBMIT ALEX/COMP/APPW {Compile all Pascal source files}
$SUBMIT ALEX/LINK/APPW {And link everything together}

F{ilemgr}D{elete}~{T11~}PHRASE
Y{es}
D{elete}~{T11~}buttons
Y{es}
D{elete}~{T11~}Obj
Y{es}Q{uit}            {Delete anything left over from prev build}

R{un}APPW/BTNREAD {Process all the APPW buttons in BTNFILE into APPW/T11buttons}
Y{es, show verbose output}


F{ilemgr}C{opy}APPW/T11buttons
~{T11~}buttons
C{opy}APPW/PREFERENCES.OBJ
~{T11~}Obj
Q{uit}

R{un}Alert {Run the alert tool to process our phrase file}
APPW/ALERTS.TEXT {Input alert file}
APPW/T11 {Output file, the PHRASE part is added automatically}

F{ilemgr}C{opy}APPW/T11PHRASE
~{T11~}PHRASE
Y{es, replace}Q{uit}
